About the Natural Event Tracker

The Natural Event Tracker is designed to help alert users to any natural disasters in their area. Through leveraging both NASA's EONET API and Twilio, we are able to create a comprehensive map of ongoing natural events, and alert users by text, if there are any in their current vicinity.

We created this website as a part of Hack the Valley 5. We hope anyone who comes along to this app enjoys it and leaves with a better understanding of natural disasters and their effect on the environment. Thank you for taking the time to visit our website.
